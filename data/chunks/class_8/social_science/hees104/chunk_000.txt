THE AGE OF COLONIALISM Before we get there, we must step back a little in time and consider the phenomenon of colonialism. Its usual definition is the practice where one country takes control of another region, establishing settlements there, and imposing its political, economic, and cultural systems. This is not a recent occurrence: colonialism can be traced to the time of the great empires in the 1% millennium BCE; in the 1* millennium CE, the spread of Christianity and Islam also involved the colonisation of the territories converted to the new faiths.

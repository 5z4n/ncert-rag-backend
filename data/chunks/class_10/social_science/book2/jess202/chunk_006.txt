Rising Importance of the Tertiary Sector in Production Over the forty years between 1977-78 and 2017-18, while production in all the three sectors has increased, it has increased the most in the tertiary sector. As a result, in the year 2017-18, the tertiary sector has emerged as the largest producing sector in India replacing the primary sector.

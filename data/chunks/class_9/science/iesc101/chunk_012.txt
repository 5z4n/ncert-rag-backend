For making this model you will need A transparent jar e A big rubber balloon or piece of stretchable rubber sheet A string e Few chickpeas or black gram or dry green peas. How to make? Put the seeds in the jar. Sew the string to the centre of the rubber sheet and put some tape to keep it tied securely. Stretch and tie the rubber sheet on the mouth of the jar. e Your model is ready. Now run your fingers up and down the string by first tugging at it slowly and then rapidly.

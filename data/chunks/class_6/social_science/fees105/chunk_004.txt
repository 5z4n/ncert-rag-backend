The names given by the ancient inhabitants of India include Jambudvipa and Bharata . The latter became widespread in time and is the name of India in most Indian languages. Foreign visitors to, or invaders of, India mostly adopted names derived from the Sindhu or Indus River; this resulted in names like Hindu , Indoi , and eventually India .
